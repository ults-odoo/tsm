Restrict Out of Stock Products in Point of Sales Odoo App

Out of Stock Product Restriction on POS Order Odoo Apps

Restrict Out of Stock Products in Sales odoo app helps users to raise out of stock warning on selecting product on pos order if the product is out of stock. Users also restrict out of stock products based on products on hand quantity or forecasted quantity and stock will check and raise warning with stock description on confirming pos order for out of stock product.

Restrict Out of Stock Product on POS Odoo App is used to configure product stock from point of sale configuration. Users can see total on hand quantity of product and also restrict product which is out of stock at the point of sale. When a user adds a product into cart which is out of stock, a warning popup rises and still they want to add product into cart click on 'Order' button.

Features

POS Configuration for Product Stock

User can configure to display stock in POS and restrict product out of stock in POS.

Display Product Stock in POS

User can see product stock quantity in green color and if product not in stock, which is in red color in POS.

Restrict Product Out of Stock in POS

User can restrict product which is out of stock in point of sale.

Raise Warning Popup if Product is Out of Stock

When user add product into cart which is out of stock, Warning popup raise.

Stock Configuration in POS

Stock Type : Qty on Hand

Under Point of Sale > Configuration > Point of Sale menu you can enable "Display Stock in POS" option to see stock for product on point of sale screen.

You can enable "Restrict Product Out of Stock in POS" option to raise warning meassge if product is out of stock on point of sale screen.

Display Product Stock Quantity in POS

You can see total on hand quantity of product, which is in green color and if product not in stock, which is in red color.

Auto Update Product Stock Quantity in POS

When you add product into cart, product stock is auto update in point of sale.

Raise Warning Popup if Add Product into Cart Which is Out of Stock

When you add product into cart, Which is out of stock, Raise Warning popup.

If product is out of stock and still you want to add product into cart click on 'Order' button.

Added Product into Cart Which is Out of Stock

You can see product added into cart which is out of stock and update product stock quantity.

Stock Type : Virtual Qty

User can select "Virtual Qty" as stock configuration option and can see virtual available quantity for product on point of sale.

Stock Type : Both

User can select "Both" as stock configuration option and can see both On Hand and Virtual available quantity for product on point of sale.
